The TVL–HE Strand is designed for students interested in cooking, baking, hospitality, tourism, caregiving, and entrepreneurship. It provides hands-on and practical training aligned with TESDA certifications.

Main Goal

  • Equip students with livelihood and service skills
  • Provide real-world employment training
  • Prepare learners for TESDA certifications
  • Develop entrepreneurial skills

What Students Learn

Cookery & Baking

Food preparation, kitchen safety, breads and pastries.

Housekeeping

Hotel procedures, cleaning standards, customer service.

Caregiving

Basic health support for children and elderly.

Entrepreneurship

Starting and managing food and service businesses.

Possible TESDA Certifications

  • Cookery NC II
  • Bread & Pastry Production NC II
  • Housekeeping NC II
  • Caregiving NC II
  • Food & Beverage Services NC II

Career Opportunities

  • Chef or Cook Assistant
  • Baker or Pastry Maker
  • Hotel or Restaurant Staff
  • Caregiver
  • Entrepreneur
